Welcome To Shariatpur Polytechnic Institute

History

Shariatpur Polytechnic Institute is the newest Polytechnic Institute in Bangladesh. It was established in 2001 with only 120 students in the first year classes of Diploma-In-Engineering in four technologies, namely, Computer, IPCT, TCT and Electronics with the growing demands of mid-level technical manpower home and abroad the Institute has since greatly expended. The Institute now offers courses in Four technologies.
